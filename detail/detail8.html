Design

Design is not just a shell, and its meaning goes far beyond functionality. It is the first language of communication between a brand and its consumers. I pursue aesthetics and sustainability in design, allowing it to stand out while conveying brand values and building emotional connections with consumers.


Plastic garbage bags cause enormous pollution to the marine environment. By redesigning the packaging of household garbage bags, the product can not only stand out visually but also strengthen the connection between the product and consumers, enhancing consumers' awareness of environmental protection.
