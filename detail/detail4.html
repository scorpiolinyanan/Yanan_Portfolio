PROJECT 4

NVSHU

(Publication
Design)
Typography
exploration

In this project, I visualize female power through a "female book" concept, blending it with typography. The flowing forms of Nüshu resemble plant roots—symbols of women's inherent strength, creativity, and resilience. Yet, born under patriarchal oppression, Nüshu also evokes thorns growing in adversity—a metaphor for female consciousness and resistance.
