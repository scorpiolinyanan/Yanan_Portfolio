Process story

Mysterious Garden Chair is an interactive exhibition at Powerhouse, featuring a cast-iron garden chair designed by Christopher Dresser in 1870. Using P5js to create a jigsaw puzzle game with patterns hidden behind the chairs, utilizing spatial screens, and providing shared experiences for designers and learners to understand the carved pattern elements and historical background of the mid-Victorian garden chair.

Design Process

The interactive experience was designed to engage visitors through a digital jigsaw puzzle that reveals the intricate patterns of the Victorian garden chair. The spatial arrangement creates an immersive environment where users can explore the historical context through modern technology.

Technical Implementation

Using P5js framework, the interactive puzzle game was developed to showcase the chair's decorative elements. The spatial screens provide a shared experience, allowing multiple users to collaborate in solving the puzzle while learning about Victorian design principles.
